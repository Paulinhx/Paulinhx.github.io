About

More About Me

Full Stack Web Developer, Painter, Mason, and Colorist in Sustainable & Historic Monument construction / Specialized in Ecologic Interior Design and Artistic Direction for more than 10 years.

Multitalented Crafter!

The story of becoming Paulinhx [pawlˈiɲks] carries on in Berlin with their Basque Occitan origins. Paulinhx as they are; non-binary, activist and sensitive with a collective mind and an indigenous soul. They are connected with the Atlantic West coast, its magical mountains, and its diverse ecology.

Creative Designer & Full Stack Web Developer with 10+ years of experience in sustainable construction and project management, who is soon to graduate from a 1+year intensive training in Full-Stack Web Development. Currently seeking to implement creative digital solutions, in order to build user oriented products and transform raw ideas into tangible sustainable & circular designs. I have over 13 years of experience working in painting, fine masonry and specializing in the renovation of ancient buildings and historic monuments with conventional and ancestral clay, gyps, and lime technics.
